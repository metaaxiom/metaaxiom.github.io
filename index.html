Karpaani English Notes
n Kerjoois to hunt
pre Ky with, following, in relation to notable use: Jored ky Woorn, first king of Karpaan, whose name means "Jored, who follows the hawk"
adj Maarni warm etymology: Proto-Abetic "Mar", sun
n Tikti star plural: "Tiktinot" vernacular: a person who is important to you, a locer or close friend
adj Voojni cold etymology: Proto-Abetic "Vajr", moon
